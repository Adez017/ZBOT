Subject: Sick Leave Application

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I am unable to attend work on [date(s)] due to illness. I woke up this morning feeling unwell and decided to consult a doctor. After examination, the doctor has advised me to take a few days off to rest and recover.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and assure you that I will make up for the lost time as soon as I am back to work. I will check my emails periodically during my leave to stay updated on any urgent matters.

Please let me know if there is any further information you require from me. I will provide a medical certificate upon my return to work.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Yours sincerely,

[Your Name]
